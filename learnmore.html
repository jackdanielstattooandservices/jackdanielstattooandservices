What are your operating hours?

Both walk-in clients and those with appointments are welcome at our studio throughout the following hours.

Do I need to make an appointment, or can I walk in for a tattoo or piercing?

Both walk-ins and appointments are accepted. Although scheduling an appointment can help ensure that you get the precise time window you want, we are available to help you anyway.

How do I book an appointment?

You can visit us in person or give our studio a call at [insert contact number] to make an appointment. Through our website, you may also request appointment information.

Do you offer aftercare instructions for tattoos and piercings?

Yes, in order to promote appropriate healing, we offer thorough aftercare guidelines for both tattoos and piercings. You'll receive advice on maintaining your new body piercing or tattoo from our artists and piercers as well.

Is it possible to get a tattoo to cover up an existing one I no longer want?

Yes, we are experts in cover-up tattoos. Bring your old tattoo to the shop so our artists can brainstorm ways to cover it up or modify it with a new design.
